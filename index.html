MARKUS HÅLAND
GRAPHIC DESIGNER
MOLDE, NORWAY
EST. 2024
MARKUS HÅLAND — MH

I'm a 19-year-old graphic designer working at Tingh kommunikasjon in Molde. My days are split between branding systems, social media content, print design (posters, rollups, t-shirts), web builds on Squarespace and Webflow, and motion design in After Effects.

Highlights include LED panel animations for Aker Stadion in Molde. My main tools are Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and Premiere Pro.

Outside of work: content creation, Valorant at Immortal rank, skating, and streetwear.
