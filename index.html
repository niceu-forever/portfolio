ABOUT
me.

MARKUS HÅLAND
GRAPHIC DESIGNER
MOLDE, NORWAY
SINCE 2025
MARKUS HÅLAND — MH

I'm 19, based in Molde, and work as a graphic designer at Tingh kommunikasjon. Most of my days involve some mix of branding, social media, print, web builds and motion — whatever the brief calls for. I use Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects and Premiere Pro.

One of my favourite projects so far has been LED panel animations for Aker Stadion. There's something pretty satisfying about seeing your work on a screen that size.

Outside of design I play Valorant at Immortal rank, skate when the weather allows, and spend too much time thinking about clothes.
