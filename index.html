About
Me

Photo goes
here

I'm Markus, a 19-year-old graphic designer at Tingh kommunikasjon in Molde, Norway. My days look like branding systems, social media visuals, poster and rollup design, Squarespace and Webflow builds, and motion work in After Effects — including LED panel animations for Molde FK stadium.

My main tools are Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and Premiere Pro. Outside of work I'm deep into content creation, Valorant, skating, Japanese streetwear, and eventually Tokyo or LA.

I care about design that doesn't look like it came out of a template generator. The kind of stuff that has a real point of view and holds up when you look at it for more than three seconds.
